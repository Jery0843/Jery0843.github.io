**Effective Date:** February 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect and how it's used on this website ("Andrés").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Analytics
We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand site traffic. This includes:
- Pages visited
- Approximate geographic region
- Browser and device type

**We do not collect personally identifiable information** through analytics.

### Comments
If you leave a comment on a post, the comment platform (e.g., Giscus/Disqus) may collect your username and profile information as per their own privacy policies.

### Payments
Premium content purchases are processed through [Whop](https://whop.com/andres-411f/). We do not store payment information — all transactions are handled by the payment platform.

## 2. Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies required for:
- Theme preference (dark/light mode)
- Basic site functionality

We do **not** use tracking cookies or sell data to third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services

This site may use the following third-party services:
- **GitHub Pages** — Hosting
- **Whop** — Payment processing
- **Giscus** — Comments (if enabled)

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs their data practices.

## 4. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data collected by third-party services is subject to their retention policies.

## 5. Your Rights

You may:
- Clear cookies from your browser at any time
- Request removal of your comments by contacting us
- Opt out of analytics by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
